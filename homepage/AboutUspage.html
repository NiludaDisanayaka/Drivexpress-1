About DriveXPress | Who We Are & What We Do

DriveXPress is the flagship automotive website of Internet Brands, which owns one of the largest networks of car buying and financing resources in Sri Lanka.

Who We Are

Our groundbreaking online car buying platform launched in 1998. Since then, CarsDirect has helped millions of consumers buy cars online and find financing with any type of credit.

As a car buying service, we've earned industry awards, such as being one of the "50 Best" websites in the world by Time Magazine, a "Top 100 Classic Website" by PC Magazine, and a "Top Site for Buying or Selling Anything" by PC World.

Today, we are often best known for our team's expertise in buying & leasing cars. We've been cited well over 4,000 times by publications like The Wall Street Journal, Fox News, Forbes, and more.

What We Do

Our team is dedicated to delivering insights you won't find anywhere else. Whether it's breaking news about cars, analyses of the latest lease deals, or in-depth comparisons of rebates and financing offers, we're passionate about helping car buyers get the best deals possible.

Our work is done by real people for real people. That means no AI-written content. We cover the good and the bad without fear or favor. That's why we're often cited by word of mouth in online forums such as Reddit and are treated as an unbiased source of car buying information.

DriveXPress also helps connect car buyers with Trusted Dealers nationwide. We also specialize in helping consumers get financed even with bad credit, no credit, or a history of bankruptcy.

How We Do It

Our prices update daily based on the latest manufacturer incentives. We also leverage a proprietary database of leasing data to bring you objective buying advice. Our online tools are meant to make it easy to build & price out any car, compare different models, and quickly get a quote with customized pricing from local dealers. We also have experienced customer service staff to help at every step.
